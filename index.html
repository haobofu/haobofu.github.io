Haobo Fu (傅浩波)

Tech Lead, Principal Researcher

Tencent AI

Haobo Fu is a principal researcher at Tencent AI Platform Department, Shenzhen, China. His main interests are new innovations in Artificial Intelligence (AI) for the game industry. These include innovative game AI applications and related research topics, such as machine learning, reinforcement learning, game theory, etc. He got his PhD in computer science from University of Birmingham in 2014, supervised by Prof. Xin Yao and Prof. Bernhard Sendhoff. His PhD program was fully funded by Honda Research Institute Europe. He was offered the Australian Government 2015 Endeavour Research Fellowship.
